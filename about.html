About Us

Oviedo Dentists Dedicated to Quality Service

Our Dentist

Dr. Michael R. Simpson, DMD has been practicing in the Oviedo area for over 20 years. He originally worked at Lockwook Dental but in 2011 founded and opened Oviedo Premier. Dr. Simpson graduated from the University of Florida, School of Dentistry and is an avid gators fan.
